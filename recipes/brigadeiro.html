Brigadeiro

Milky chocolate desert

A very popular desert in Brazil, done with some really basic ingredients.

Can also be done in a lot of different ways, have fun!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Can of Condensed Milk
  • 3 full tbl spoons of chocolate powder
  • 1 tbl spoon of butter

Steps:

  1. Add all the condensed milk together with the chocolate powder into a pan
  2. Mix in low to medium heat until it starts to thicken
  3. Once its easy to see the bottle of the pan, add the butter
  4. Turn off the heat, and mix it well
  5. Wait for it to cool down, and serve :)
