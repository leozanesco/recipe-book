Lamen with Eggs

Fast Lamen with well-done eggs

A fast and warm dish, great for cold nights!

Can be done with any type of Lamen, and you can add all garnishes you wish!

Ingredients:

  • Lamen Noodles
  • Lamen seasoning of your choice
  • 3 Eggs
  • Parsley or another herb of your choice

Steps:

  1. Add 250ml of water to two pans, and heat one of em
  2. Once the water is boiling, carefully add the 3 Eggs
  3. After 6 minutes, turn off the fire and change the water for the coldest you can have
    • This stops the cooking process of the egg, so it doesn't overcook
  4. Turn on the heat on the second pan
  5. While waiting for the water to boil, you can peel the egg shells
  6. Add the Noodles and boil them for 3 minutes
  7. Once done, add the seasoning of your choice and mix it well
  8. Move it to a bowl, and add the eggs cut in half
  9. Season eggs to taste
  10. Add Parsley or any other garnish
